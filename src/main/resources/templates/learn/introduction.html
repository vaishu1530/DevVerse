What is C Language?

C is a powerful, efficient, and fast programming language that forms the foundation for many modern languages like C++, Java, and Python. It was developed in the early 1970s by Dennis Ritchie at Bell Labs.

C is a **procedural programming language** known for its simplicity, performance, and close relationship to the hardware. Because of its speed and control, C is often used in system-level programming such as operating systems, embedded devices, compilers, and more.
